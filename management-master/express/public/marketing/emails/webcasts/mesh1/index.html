Mesh is an enterprise platform for the Internet of Things. The platform consists of a proprietary application layer running on a low-cost, low-power chip the size of your fingernail with advanced networking capabilities. Mesh runs multiple applications simultaneously and like a smart phone, you can add, delete, or update any application independently over-the-air, without disrupting other running applications in the process.

Mesh is currently raising $2 million in an equity round, to continue developing their technology and to move forward with initial pilot contracts.

Dealflow is hosting a private webcast for Mesh on Tuesday, December 9th at 2:00pm (ET)

Some key facts about Mesh:

  • Mesh’s platform addresses issues like security and flexibility that stand in the way of Enterprise and Industrial customers adopting IoT technologies.
  • Mesh’s proprietary application layer enables developers to easily create IoT applications.
  • Mesh’s platform is easy to integrate with third-party hardware and software.
  • The platform is hardware agnostic, allowing it to be ported to any chip type on the market meeting minimum specifications.
  • Initial market opportunities include correctional facilities to increase tracking capabilities for correctional officers and prisoners and a solution to increase parking efficiencies in existing garages.
