THE NEW ERA OF DEAL MARKETING
Equity Crowdfunding and the Advent of Online Deal Marketing

Dealflow's latest whitepaper provides you with extraordinary insight into online deal-marketing trends and cuts through all the noise about equity crowdfunding to explain what it all means for you and your business.

The use of technology to efficiently pitch investments to the public is a game changer that has far-reaching consequences for both investors and companies raising capital. New regulations now allow for broad solicitation of deals and traditional methods of capital formation are quickly being replaced by those that leverage social media.

What's inside this white paper…

Crowdfunding has worked its way into the investment lexicon over the past few years but really, the practical application of crowdfunding today has excluded the crowd and been limited to accredited investors only. In this white paper, we explain what accredited-only crowdfunding can do for you today, and we illustrate online deal marketing trends in a way that’s useful to your business now.




Equity crowdfunding is still in its early stages. Dealflow has been engaged in this industry since its first glimmer in 2010, and we believe that we’re in the first inning of what promises to be an extraordinary ball game. Much remains to be seen about the legal and financial implications of letting Main Street into Wall Street’s backyard, but there’s no question that crowdfunding represents another example of how technology will change the way we do things.
